We are Student Shala, a platform dedicated to simplifying the search for student accommodations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate the stress and uncertainty of finding a place to live for students moving to a new city. We provide a curated list of verified paying guest (PG) accommodations, complete with transparent pricing, genuine reviews, and essential amenities. We aim to create a community where students can not only find a room but a place they can truly call home.
